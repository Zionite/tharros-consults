Services Offered

We are a career and business coaching company that’s keen on helping individuals and startups scale up.

Services Offered:

  • Career coaching: Whether you are still studying, fresh out of college, or looking to switch careers, we are here to listen and hold your hand through the process. The goal is simple: get you to your goal.
  • Academic admission & scholarship coaching: From choosing the best fit in terms of course options, reviewing your statement of purpose (SOP), and more, we coach prospective students to help position you for undergraduate & postgraduate admission and scholarship.
  • Startup business consulting: For startups and small to mid-scale businesses, we offer tailored financial and strategy advisory services.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower our clients to discover and utilize their strengths, enabling them to excel in their professional lives. We believe in a holistic approach to career development, focusing on both personal and professional growth.
